LATEST NEWS ABOUT US

  • ISO 9001-2015 Certification

    Flocentra Aviation Pvt Ltd is proud to announce that we have been awarded the ISO 9001-2015 certification. This prestigious certification demonstrates our commitment to consistently providing high-quality services that meet customer and regulatory requirements. Our focus on continuous improvement and operational excellence ensures that our clients receive the best possible service in the aviation industry.

  • Membership in International Operators Representatives Association (IORA)

    We are delighted to share that Flocentra Aviation Pvt Ltd has been accepted as a member of the International Operators Representatives Association (IORA). This membership enhances our global reach and allows us to collaborate with industry leaders, further strengthening our position as a trusted flight support partner.

  • Requisite Approvals from Key Ministries and Authorities

    Flocentra Aviation Pvt Ltd has achieved all necessary credentials and approvals from the Indian Government and relevant authorities, including the Ministry of Corporate Affairs, Ministry of Civil Aviation, and Directorate General of Civil Aviation (DGCA). These approvals enable us to operate at full capacity, ensuring seamless and efficient flight support services across India.

  • Established Operational Control Center (OCC)

    We have successfully established a 24x7 Operational Control Center (OCC) to provide a single point of contact and control for all operational needs of our clients in India. This facility ensures timely and efficient handling of trip planning, flight permits, and ground services, providing our clients with a hassle-free experience and the highest level of service quality.
