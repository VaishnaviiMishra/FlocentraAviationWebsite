About Us

Founded on xx/xx/xx

Flocentra Aviation Pvt Ltd is a private limited company providing comprehensive aviation support services for international and domestic flights at major Indian airports. Our goal is to significantly contribute to the global aviation service industry by delivering high-quality inputs and fostering growth.

We strive to be a leading provider of supervision and ground handling support for non-scheduled business jets, charters, VIP flights, and ambulance flights by adopting the best international standards and practices. Our focus is on fulfilling client commitments with precision, ensuring optimal resource utilization, and offering services at competitive prices.

Our skilled and qualified team guarantees that all flight support needs are met under one roof, delivering exemplary and timely assistance with a red carpet treatment.

Vision

"Quality service aiming for perfection, meeting all commitments, and delivering the right service at the right time and place."

Mission

"Commitment is an action, not just a word. It transforms dreams into reality, accepting no excuses, only results." Flocentra Aviation Pvt Ltd is dedicated to turning every promise into reality.

"Quality is defined by what the customer gains and values. It is not about the difficulty or cost of production." Flocentra Aviation Pvt Ltd consistently provides products and services that meet customer and regulatory requirements.

"Perfection is achieved by identifying and improving imperfections. Success is about minimizing and accommodating imperfections." By striving for continuous improvement, Flocentra Aviation Pvt Ltd aims for excellence.
