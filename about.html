About Us

Flight handling More

Flocentra Aviation Pvt Ltd has been incorporated as private limited company with the aim of providing entire spectrum of Aviation support services for international/domestic flights across all major airports in India to contribute substantially in the growing global aviation service industry by providing quality inputs and becoming part of the growth.

We aim to be a leading supervision and ground handling support provider for non-schedule business jets, charters, VIP flights and ambulance flights by cultivating and adapting the best international standards and practice as a habitat environment within the organization. We are focused on achieving all commitments made to the client aiming perfection and delivering right service at right time and right place at best prize, ensuring optimal utilization of resources.
