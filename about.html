About Us

Founded on 02 Aug 2021

We proudly introduce Flocentra Aviation Pvt Ltd, a start-up company incorporated three years ago. While we may not have a long history of success and achievements, we are dedicated to delivering the highest quality of service. Our commitment to excellence is matched by our focus on cost-effectiveness for our valued clients through optimal resource utilization. Managed by our expert and experienced team, we have already demonstrated significant growth and success in a short period. We eagerly look forward to further opportunities to prove our capabilities.

Flocentra Aviation Pvt Ltd has obtained all necessary credentials from the Indian Government and relevant authorities to operate at full capacity.

We have secured approvals from several ministries, including the Ministry of Corporate Affairs, Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises, Ministry of Tourism, Ministry of Finance, Ministry of Civil Aviation, Ministry of Commerce and Industry (Directorate General of Foreign Trade), Directorate General of Civil Aviation (DGCA), Bureau of Civil Aviation Security (BCAS), and the Government of the National Capital Territory of Delhi. Additionally, we are a member of the International Operators Representatives Association (IORA) and hold the ISO 9001-2015 certification. Verification details can be found in the attached documents.

Brief description of the Company:

Flocentra Aviation Pvt Ltd was founded by Mrs. Sudha Mishra and Mr. Dhiraj Mishra, dynamic and seasoned professionals with nearly three decades of rich experience in aviation and security. Our company values expert knowledge and quick responses to every requirement of the aviation industry and our prestigious clients. We deliver all our services through a highly skilled and qualified team of officers and trusted partners. We ensure that all your flight support needs are fulfilled under one roof by experienced and qualified professionals, providing exemplary and timely assistance with a red carpet treatment.

Methodology to achieve best quality and cost effectiveness in services:

We operate as an authorized representative, agent, or nodal agency to represent, coordinate, and manage the complete trip, permissions, planning, and operation of your flights (inbound and outbound) at multiple airport locations. We fulfill and manage all your requirements during your flight trip into India, providing you with a single point of contact and control under one roof. This approach ensures your entire trip is highly cost-effective through optimal resource utilization managed by our highly skilled and qualified team of officers and trusted partners.
