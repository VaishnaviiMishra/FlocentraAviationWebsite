Flocentra Aviation Private

Your Exclusive Ally for Flight Support in India

Our company, founded by Mrs. Sudha Mishra and Mr. Dhiraj Mishra, is dedicated to providing exceptional services to the aviation industry both domestically and internationally. Our vision is to cultivate and adapt the best international standards and practices, creating a thriving organizational environment. We are committed to fulfilling all client commitments with precision, delivering the right service at the right time and place, and at the best price, while ensuring optimal resource utilization.

Services we Provide

Flight Clearance and Permit

Securing necessary authorizations and permits for international and domestic flights to ensure compliance with aviation regulations.

Ground Handling

Offering comprehensive ground support services, including baggage handling, aircraft marshaling, and passenger assistance.

Cargo Handling

Managing the safe and efficient loading, unloading, and transportation of cargo, ensuring timely delivery.

Airport Handling

Providing a range of services at the airport, including check-in, security, boarding assistance, and more.

Training

Delivering specialized training programs for aviation personnel to enhance skills and maintain safety standards.

Air Charters Services

Offering tailored charter flight services for private, corporate, and group travel needs.

Air Ambulance

Providing emergency medical air transport services with fully equipped aircraft and trained medical staff.

Aviation Fuel

Supplying high-quality aviation fuel to aircraft, ensuring reliable and efficient refueling services.

Tour and Travel

Arranging comprehensive travel packages, including flights, accommodations, and itineraries for both leisure and business travelers.

Flight Planning

Developing detailed flight plans to optimize routes, ensure safety, and comply with regulatory requirements.

Trip Support

Offering a range of support services for flights, including catering, ground transportation, and accommodation arrangements.

Operation Management

Overseeing the day-to-day operations of aviation activities, ensuring efficiency, safety, and regulatory compliance.

Auxiliary Services to Airlines

Providing additional support services to airlines, including maintenance, catering, and customer service assistance.
